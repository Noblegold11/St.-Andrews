Privacy & Data Protection

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Information Collection

St. Andrews Global Preparatory collects personal data through admissions inquiries, enrollment forms, and secure payment processing. This includes student academic records, parental contact information, and financial identifiers required for tuition settlement.

2. Use of Information

Data is used solely for educational placement, administrative communication, and secure financial transactions. We do not sell or lease institutional data to third-party marketing entities.

3. Data Security

As seen in our secure payment portal, we utilize AES-256 bit encryption for all sensitive transmissions. Our internal databases are restricted to authorized faculty with verified credentials.

For inquiries regarding data erasure or access, please contact the Registrar's Office at registrar@standrews.edu.
